🏃‍♂️ Storytelling of a Curious Student's Step Analysis 📊

An Adventure in Data Discovery 🔍

Once upon a time, in a bustling college campus 🏫, there was a curious student who spent his days walking between various locations - from lecture halls to the mess, tech cafe to ED classes, and numerous labs scattered across the campus.

One day, while exploring his phone's features 📱, he stumbled upon an incredible discovery: an inbuilt application that had been silently tracking his daily activities, including:

  • 👣 Step Count
  • ⏱️ Duration
  • 📏 Distance
  • 🔥 Calories Burned
