Step Count Analysis 📈

📊 To kick things off, he crafted a histogram showcasing his step count distribution, grouping them into bins of 2500 steps. The results? A fascinating spread of activity across the month! 🚶‍♂️📅

The chart revealed an interesting pattern—some days were super active, while others had significantly fewer steps. This balance suggests a mix of energetic and relaxed days. ⚖️📈

Overall, his step count seemed to be well-distributed, indicating a dynamic lifestyle with alternating levels of physical activity. 🏃‍♂️

🏠 Home vs Hostel: Step Count Insights 🚶‍♂️📊

Ever wondered how your surroundings impact your activity levels? 🤔 Well, I did—and the results were eye-opening! 👀

📝 When I was at home, I naturally walked less—maybe it was the cozy vibes or easy access to everything. 🏡🛋️

🚀 But in the hostel, my step count skyrocketed! 🎉 Whether it was running to the mess 🍽️, climbing stairs 🏃‍♂️, or just wandering around with friends, the difference was huge! 🏆

To visualize this, I tracked the number of days spent in each location and used two different colors in my scatter plot to distinguish between my home and hostel days. 🎨📊

🔍 The contrast is fascinating—where you live can shape your daily movement patterns more than you think! 💡🗝️
